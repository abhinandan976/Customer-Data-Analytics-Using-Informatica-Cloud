Customer Data Analytics with Informatica Cloud

Transform raw customer data into actionable insights with our cutting-edge ETL pipeline.

Project Overview

In today’s data-driven landscape, businesses often struggle with scattered, incomplete, and inconsistent customer data coming from multiple platforms like CRM systems, e-commerce portals, support systems, and marketing tools.

Our project, Customer Data Analytics using Informatica Cloud, is aimed at addressing this challenge by building a robust and scalable ETL pipeline using Informatica to transform raw customer data into actionable insights. The solution consolidates raw customer data from different domains into a unified view suitable for analytics ready format.

Objectives

  • To extract customer data from CRM, e-commerce, support, and marketing systems.
  • To clean, transform, and standardize data using Informatica Cloud transformations.
  • To integrate datasets into a unified schema for deeper behavioral analysis.
  • To compute customer metrics like total purchases, support interactions, and campaign responses.
  • To improve data accessibility for business intelligence and decision-making.
